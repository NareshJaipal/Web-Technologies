Overview

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) degree program prepares students to become qualified educators. The program emphasizes educational theory, child development, curriculum design, classroom management, and assessment.

Outcomes

Graduates of the Bachelor of Education program will be able to:
  • Demonstrate a deep understanding of child development and learning theories.
  • Design effective lesson plans and instructional strategies for diverse learners.
  • Create a positive and supportive classroom environment.
  • Utilize various assessment methods to measure student learning and guide instruction.

Jobs

The B.Ed degree prepares graduates for teaching roles in various settings, including early childhood, elementary, and secondary education. Explore potential career paths in education:
  • Early Childhood Educator
  • Elementary School Teacher
  • Middle School Teacher
  • High School Teacher
  • Special Education Teacher
